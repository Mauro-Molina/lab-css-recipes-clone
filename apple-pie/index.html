Apple Pie

This was my grandmothre is pie recipe. I have never seen another one quite like it. It will always be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!

Ingredients

  • - recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • - 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • - 3 tablespons all-purpose flour
  • - 1/4 cup watter
  • - 3 tablespons all-purpose flour
  • - 1/4 cup watter
  • - 1/2 cup white sugar
  • - 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

1) Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 deg'ees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to from a paste. Add watter, white sugar and brown sugar, and bring to a boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer

2) Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not to run

3) Bake 15 minutesin the preheated oven. Reduce the Temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Continue backing for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft
